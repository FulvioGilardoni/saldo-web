Privacy Policy

App: Saldo  ·  Developer: Fulvio Gilardoni / fgstack  ·  Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Saldo ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Saldo mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile photo (if you sign in with Google or Apple).
  • Usage data: expenses you enter, group information, recurring expense templates, and settlement records.
  • Subscription data: your subscription plan (Free or Plus), purchase status, and expiry date. We never store raw payment card details — purchases are processed entirely by Apple or Google.
  • Device information: FCM token for push notifications, OS type (iOS/Android) for IAP receipt verification.
  • Crash reports: anonymised crash logs collected by Firebase Crashlytics (only in production builds).
  • Analytics events: anonymised in-app events (e.g. paywall shown, feature used) collected by Firebase Analytics to improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and synchronise the App in real time across your devices.
  • To send push notifications about new expenses, payments, and recurring reminders (only with your permission).
  • To verify in-app purchases and grant access to Saldo Plus features.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability.
  • To understand which features are used most so we can improve the product.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore database), hosted in the EU (europe-west1 region). Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): database, authentication, push notifications, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Apple / Google: in-app purchase receipt verification.
  • Group members: your display name and profile photo are visible to members of groups you join or create.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the App, all your personal data — including profile, expenses, groups, and subscription records — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account in the App. For other requests, contact us at the address below.

7. Children

Saldo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
